# 🧬 BodyLab — Human Body Analyser

> Know your body like never before. BodyLab gives you a full health breakdown — from BMI to meal plans to daily vitamins — personalised just for you.

---

## 🌐 Live Demo

🔗 **[View Live Site](https://farmdirect-jeeva.github.io/bodylab/)**

---

## ✨ Features

- 📊 9 Core Body Metrics — BMI, ideal weight, TDEE, body fat %, BMR, protein, water, carbs, sleep
- 💊 12 Vitamins & Minerals — personalised daily doses by age and gender
- 🥩 Protein-Rich Foods Guide — Animal, Plant-Based, Dairy & Snacks
- 🍊 How to Get Vitamins from Food — best natural sources + absorption tips
- 🍽️ Personalised Daily Meal Plan — 5 meals adapted to your goal
- ⏰ Ideal Daily Routine — hour-by-hour schedule
- 💡 Personalised Health Tips — dynamic insights based on your data
- ✅ Daily Self-Care Checklist — trackable habits with progress bar

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- Pure HTML, CSS & JavaScript
- Zero dependencies, no frameworks, no backend
- Google Fonts — Clash Display · Cabinet Grotesk · Martian Mono
- localStorage for checklist persistence

---

## 📐 Formulas Used

| Metric | Formula |
|---|---|
| BMR | Mifflin-St Jeor equation |
| TDEE | BMR × Activity multiplier |
| Ideal Weight | Devine formula |
| Body Fat % | BMI-based estimation |
| Water Intake | 35ml × body weight (kg) |
| Protein | Goal-adjusted 1.6–2.0g per kg |

## 🚀 Getting Started

No installation needed — single HTML file.

1. Download `index.html`
2. Open in any browser
3. Enter your details and hit Analyse!
